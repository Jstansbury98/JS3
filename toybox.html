Toybox Profiles

Toybox Circus has been running for around 15 years and has had members from all areas in Chorlton. We strive to deliver a professional but also fun service. Some of our members over the years have gone on to become circus professionals themselves and have had students leave us to go train at The National Centre for Circus Arts as well as Rotterdam Circus School.

Our staff teach primarily manipulation (hand-held) skills such as Feather Balancing, Plate Spinning, Juggling, Diabolo, Flower Sticks, Hula Hoop, and Poi, but also equilibristic (balance) skills from Unicycling through to Stilts, Rolla Bolla, and Acrobalance.

Through a mixture of games and group teaching, young people are able to share in the fun of learning circus in a safe, controlled space. We encourage peer teaching and have 3 members of staff who started as members of our Youth Circus. Circus can be silly and serious, but we keep the silly out of the serious to ensure all young people understand the safety elements of any skills that we teach to ensure that accidents are minimised.

Martin

Unicyclist, Juggler, Youthworker, Scouter, Family Woodworker, Veggie, Trained by Owen, Ex-Bike Mechanic, Environmentalist.

Martin comes from a scouting family background, and this was where he had his first taste of circus. Later in life, as an adult, Martin became immersed in the world of poi spinning and started to attend juggling conventions, where he began practicing and learning new skills. This led him to perform at events, where he met Owen Gaynor and was offered a role teaching at Toybox due to the former member of staff starting a tour with Nofit State Circus.

After a number of years serving as Toybox's support staff, the opportunity was given to lead the Toybox sessions and be Unity's main circus staff member. Martin now has a team of four staff whose skills are broad and varied to fit all aspects of circus teaching at Toybox.

Pete

Youthworker, Juggler, Yoga Practitioner, Contact Juggler, Deep Knowledge of Juggling History.

Pete has 20+ years of experience as a juggler and youthworker. He holds an in-depth knowledge of circus history and theory, making him a remarkable resource for circus tuition. Combining the knowledge of circus with physical skill means we can truly help our young people grow both mentally and physically. Pete is a yoga practitioner and manages to bring this focus into his teaching of contact juggling and other skills.

Morgan

Ryan

Unicyclist, Juggler, Acrobalance Base, Molly's Acro Partner, Works in the Events Industry, Toybox Young Person.
